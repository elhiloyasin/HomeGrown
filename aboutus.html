About Home Grown

Shop small. Win big.

As a result of Covid-19's devastating impact on small and locally owned businesses, we wanted to find a way to help support them. At Home Grown, you can easily find local businesses within your area based on what you need. You can also stay in the know for trendy and unique locations by visiting our "Featured" page. For newsworthy articles related to boutique business and its industry, look no further than our "Blog" page.

Home Grown was designed and orchestrated by "The Creatives" comprised of Satyam, Terry, Yasin, and Kokhob for George Washington University's Full Stack Web Development program. With a focus on front end web development, our website was constructed using the following: HTML, CSS, CSS frameworks, Javascript, APIs, and featured content from third-party blogs.
